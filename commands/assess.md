## Assessment Results

Once you've completed an assessment:

1. **Score Your Results**: Mark your understanding level
2. **Identify Gaps**: Focus on incomplete areas
3. **Create Action Plan**: Use `/projects` for practice
4. **Track Progress**: Re-assess every 2-4 weeks

## Scoring Guide

- **Beginner**: 0-25% knowledge in this category
- **Intermediate**: 25-75% knowledge in this category
- **Advanced**: 75-100% knowledge in this category
